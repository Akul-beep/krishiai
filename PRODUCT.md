# Krishi AI

## What it is

Krishi AI is a single-scroll marketing site for an IoT-based precision agriculture platform. The hardware unit combines NPK, soil moisture, soil temperature, and ambient temperature/humidity sensors with solar power, real-time weather APIs, and AI-driven recommendations for irrigation, fertilizer, pest risk, and crop disease.

## Audience

Indian farmers, agricultural cooperatives, NGOs, impact investors, and pilot partners — especially in Punjab (Fazilka pilot).

## Register

Brand (marketing landing page; design is the product).

## Goals

- Lead with impact numbers (30% less water, 20% yield increase, 20% less fertilizer)
- Show field evidence (workshops, farmer interviews, photos)
- Explain the IoT product and AI recommendations
- Communicate roadmap (Fazilka pilot → expand → partner)
- Align with SDGs 2, 6, and 13
- Introduce founder (Akul Nehra) and team (Karambir Inder Singh, COO)
